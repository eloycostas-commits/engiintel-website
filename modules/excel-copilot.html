Capabilities

Everything you need to work with Excel data,
without the Excel complexity.

Engineers without advanced Excel skills get any result they need. Just describe what you want in plain language.

💬
Natural Language Queries
Ask questions about your Excel data in plain Spanish or English. Get instant tables with filtered, sorted, and aggregated results.
"Show me elevators with 1966 regulations by region"
→ Instant filtered table with results
📊
One-Command Chart Generation
Generate bar charts, line charts, and pie charts with a single command. The AI creates the chart and embeds it in a downloadable Excel file.
"Create a bar chart showing elevators by region"
→ Chart generated and ready to download
💾
Macro System
Save frequent queries as macros with one click. Reuse them instantly without retyping. Share macros with your team for consistent results.
Click "Save as Macro" → Name it → One-click reuse
Perfect for recurring reports

Workflow Automation
Define custom workflows with business logic. The AI follows your rules automatically for consistent, repeatable analysis.
WORKFLOW: Filter by region → Filter by regulation → Count
→ AI applies your logic every time

Multi-Sheet Analysis
Query across multiple sheets in the same workbook. The AI understands relationships and correlates data automatically.
"Compare data from Sheet1 and Sheet2"
→ Cross-sheet analysis in seconds

100% Local Processing
All Excel analysis runs on your server. No data sent to external APIs. Full GDPR compliance by design.
Your data never leaves your infrastructure
→ Complete privacy and control
