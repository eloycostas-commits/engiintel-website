Process

From document to answer
in seconds.

No complex setup. No training required. Upload your documents, ask your question, get your answer.

01
Deploy on Your Server
IT runs docker-compose up. The full stack starts automatically on your hardware.
02
Upload Documents
Upload PDFs, scanned documents or Excel files. EngiIntel processes and indexes them using local AI.
03
Ask in Natural Language
Engineers type their question in plain language. The system searches semantically across all indexed documents.
04
Get Cited Answers
Receive precise answers with document name and page number. Verify in the source in one click.
