Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

EngiIntel ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our platform at engiintel.com. We process data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

EngiIntel is the data controller for personal data collected through this website and the platform. If you have any questions about data processing, contact us at hello@engiintel.com.

2. Data We Collect

Category Examples How collected
Account data Name, email address, GitHub username Registration / GitHub OAuth
Usage data Pages visited, features used, query history Automatically via cookies and logs
Document content PDFs, Excel files, images you upload Uploaded by you
Communication data Messages sent via contact form Submitted by you
Technical data IP address, browser type, device information Automatically via server logs

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Service delivery – to authenticate you, process documents, and return AI-generated results
  • Service improvement – to understand how the platform is used and improve its features
  • Communications – to send account and security notifications, and (with consent) product updates
  • Legal compliance – to comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract performance – processing necessary to deliver the service you requested
  • Legitimate interests – improving platform security, preventing abuse, and product analytics
  • Consent – marketing communications (you can withdraw consent at any time)
  • Legal obligation – when required by applicable law

5. Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) with Consent Mode v2. Analytics cookies are only set after you accept our cookie notice. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. You can manage cookie preferences via the cookie banner or your browser settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Infrastructure providers – Supabase (database), Vercel (hosting), Groq (AI inference) — all under data processing agreements
  • Analytics – Google Analytics (anonymised usage data)
  • Law enforcement – when required by law or to protect our legal rights

All processors are contractually bound to use data only for the purposes we specify.

7. Document Data

Documents you upload are stored in encrypted form for the sole purpose of powering the search and AI query features. During the Beta period, documents may be retained until your account is deleted. We do not use your documents to train AI models.

8. Data Retention

We retain account data for as long as your account is active. Usage logs are retained for up to 12 months. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@engiintel.com.

9. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting prior lawful processing)
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk)

To exercise any right, email hello@engiintel.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. International Transfers

Some processors (e.g. Groq for AI inference) operate in the United States. Where data is transferred outside the UK/EEA, we ensure adequate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the ICO.

11. Security

We implement technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including encryption at rest and in transit, access controls, and regular security reviews. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Children

The platform is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notice. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
