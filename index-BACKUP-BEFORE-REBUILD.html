Frequently Asked Questions

What does 'on-premise' mean?
EngiIntel runs entirely on your own servers. No data is sent to external cloud services. All AI processing happens locally on your infrastructure.
How is this different from ChatGPT or Claude?
ChatGPT and Claude are cloud-based services that require sending your data to external servers. EngiIntel runs on your infrastructure, ensuring zero data leakage. Plus, EngiIntel is specifically designed for technical documentation with features like page-level citations, OCR, and asset management.
What are the system requirements?
Minimum: 16GB RAM, 4 CPU cores, 100GB storage. Recommended: 32GB RAM, 8 CPU cores, 500GB SSD. Works on any server with Docker support (Linux, Windows Server, macOS).
How long does deployment take?
Initial deployment takes 10-15 minutes with a single Docker command. Document indexing time depends on your document volume (typically 1-2 hours for 1000 documents).
Can I try it before buying?
Yes! The Free plan is forever free with up to 100 documents and 1 user. For Pro features, we offer a 30-day free trial with no credit card required.
What languages are supported?
The interface supports English and Spanish. Document processing works with any language, though accuracy is highest for English, Spanish, French, German, and Italian.
Do you offer training?
Yes. Pro plans include video tutorials and documentation. Enterprise plans include dedicated onboarding sessions and custom training for your team.
What file formats are supported?
PDF (native and scanned), Word (DOC, DOCX), Excel (XLS, XLSX), PowerPoint (PPT, PPTX), images (PNG, JPG), and plain text. OCR automatically processes scanned documents.
Can I integrate with SharePoint or Google Drive?
Yes. Pro and Enterprise plans include document connectors for SharePoint, Google Drive, and Confluence. Documents sync automatically.
Is there a limit on queries?
No. All plans include unlimited queries. Since everything runs on your infrastructure, there are no per-query API costs.
What about updates and maintenance?
Updates are delivered as Docker images. You control when to update. Pro plans include update notifications. Enterprise plans include managed updates and rollback support.
Can I export my data?
Yes. All your data is stored in standard formats (PostgreSQL database, file storage). You can export everything at any time. No vendor lock-in.
What support options are available?
Free: Community forum. Pro: Email support (24h response time). Enterprise: Dedicated support engineer, phone support, SLA guarantee.
Do you offer custom development?
Yes. Enterprise plans can include custom integrations, specialized modules, and tailored workflows. Contact us to discuss your requirements.
What is your refund policy?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your payment in full, no questions asked.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes. You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.
