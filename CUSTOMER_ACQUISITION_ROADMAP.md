## RISK MITIGATION

### Risk 1: No Demo Requests
**Mitigation:**
- Offer free pilot program (no credit card)
- Create 5-minute demo video
- Add live chat to website
- Reduce friction: "Book demo" → "Watch demo"

### Risk 2: Trials Don't Convert
**Mitigation:**
- Improve onboarding (setup calls, tutorials)
- Add usage tracking (show value: "You saved X hours")
- Offer extended trial (60 days instead of 30)
- Add money-back guarantee

### Risk 3: Wrong Target Market
**Mitigation:**
- Test multiple ICPs (elevator, manufacturing, consultancies)
- Track which industries convert best
- Double down on winners
- Pivot messaging based on feedback

### Risk 4: Price Too High
**Mitigation:**
- Offer annual discount (€490/year = 2 months free)
- Add free tier (freemium model)
- Show ROI calculator prominently
- Emphasize cost savings vs cloud AI
